revolution about the sun of 88.0 days at a mean distance of 58.3 million kilometers (36.2
million miles) and a mean radius of approximately 2,424 kilometers (1,500 miles).
Venus
The second planet from the sun, having an average radius of 6,052 kilometers (3,760
miles), a mass 0.815 times that of Earth, and a sideral period of revolution about the sun of
224.7 days at a mean distance of approximately 108.1 million kilometers (67.2 million
miles).
