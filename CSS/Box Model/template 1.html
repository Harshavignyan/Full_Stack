Suzume

A fantastic anime movie released
in 2023 april, won lot of positive
reviews both from fans
and critics.As the skies turn red and
the planet trembles, Japan stands on the
brink of disaster. However, a determined teenager
named Suzume sets out on a mission to save her
country. Able to see supernatural forces
that others cannot, it's up to her to
close the mysterious doors that are spreading
chaos across the land. A perilous
journey awaits as the fate of Japan
rests on her shoulders.
